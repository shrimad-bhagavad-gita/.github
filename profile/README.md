## 🌟 About this Project

Welcome to the **Shrimad Bhagavad Gita** open-source organization — a digital companion for studying and exploring the timeless teachings of the Bhagavad Gita.

The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred dialogue between **Arjuna** and **Lord Krishna**, spoken on the battlefield of **Kurukshetra** over 5,000 years ago. Its teachings continue to guide people through challenges, purpose, discipline, devotion, and self-discovery.

Our mission is to make this ancient wisdom:

- 📱 Accessible to everyone
- 🔍 Easy to explore and search
- 🤖 Enhanced with modern technology
- 🌍 Open-source for the global community

# ✨ Features

Explore all **700 verses** of the Bhagavad Gita organized beautifully with:
- Sanskrit slokas
- Transliteration
- Translations
- Meanings and explanations

Instantly search across the entire Gita by:

- Topics
- Meanings

## 🧠 Topic Groups
Find verses grouped by real-life themes such as:

## 🔊 Text to Speech
